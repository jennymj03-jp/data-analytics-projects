# Customer Feedback Dashboard – Power BI Project

## Project Overview

The Customer Feedback Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze hotel guest feedback, customer satisfaction trends, Net Promoter Score (NPS), and customer engagement patterns.

The dashboard provides business insights into customer ratings, visit purposes, source effectiveness, gender distribution, and feedback trends to support data-driven hospitality management decisions.

---

## Objectives

* Monitor customer satisfaction and feedback performance
* Analyze NPS categories and guest sentiment
* Identify major customer acquisition sources
* Evaluate guest behavior and visit purposes
* Track feedback trends over time
* Improve customer experience through data insights

## Dashboard Features

### Overall Rating Analysis

* Displays average customer rating
* Tracks overall hotel service quality

### NPS Score Monitoring

* Measures customer loyalty and satisfaction
* Categorizes users into:

  * Promoters
  * Passive
  * Detractors

### Purpose of Visit Analysis

* Business
* Vacation
* Other travel purposes

### Customer Segmentation

* Gender-based analysis
* Visitor distribution tracking

### Feedback Category Analysis

* Facility ratings
* Restaurant ratings
* Room ratings
* Staff ratings

### Source of Information Analysis

Tracks customer acquisition channels such as:

* Hotel booking sites
* Search engines
* Internet advertisements
* Word of mouth
* Television advertisements
* Newspapers

### Trend Analysis

* Visualizes customer feedback trends over time
* Identifies seasonal engagement patterns
